⭑ Editor's Pick — College Sports

Capital University Baseball
vs. Wilmington — Doubleheader

📍 Huntington Park, Downtown Columbus 🕐 Wednesday Apr 8 Free · Donation appreciated
Huntington Park on a weeknight with the Clippers out of town seats 10,000 people. With Capital University playing a doubleheader, you'll have your pick of seats behind home plate that would cost you $40 at a Clippers game. These are DIII athletes — serious ballplayers — competing on the same field, same scoreboard, same grass. Last time we checked, admission was free with a donation bucket if you feel like it. Two games, probably nothing out of pocket, one of the best ballparks in the midwest. Get there early and stay for both.
