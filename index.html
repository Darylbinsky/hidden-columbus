OSU Men's Gymnastics:
Big Ten Dual Meet

📍 Covelli Center, OSU Campus 🕐 7:00 PM Free with student ID · $5 general
"We stumbled in not knowing what to expect and left completely blown away. These kids are world-class athletes performing 15 feet in front of you — no jumbotron filter, no $18 beers. ⚔️ Combat Sports
★★★★☆
Buhurt Tournament — Logan High School
Logan High School Field House, Logan, OH
Full-contact medieval armored combat, right here in central Ohio. Actual steel, actual hits. You've never seen anything like this at a high school. Bring the whole family — kids go absolutely wild for it and admission is minimal. This is the most surprising thing you'll see all year.

OSU School of Music — Free Faculty Recital
Weigel Hall, OSU Campus
World-class classical and chamber music, performed by working professionals. Routinely better than ticketed shows at the Riffe. No charge, no reservation needed, half-empty rooms. One of the great free things in this city that nobody talks about.
